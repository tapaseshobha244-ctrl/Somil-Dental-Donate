Treatments & Pricing

Transparent, affordable dental care. All consultations include a thorough examination and personalised treatment plan.

General Dental Checkup

Starting from ₹100

Comprehensive oral examination with digital X-rays to detect cavities, gum disease, and other issues early. Preventive care at its best.

Teeth Cleaning (Scaling)

Starting from ₹500

Professional scaling and polishing to remove stubborn plaque and tartar buildup, leaving your teeth smooth, fresh, and healthy. Recommended every 6 months.

Tooth Filling

Starting from ₹500

Tooth-coloured composite or silver amalgam fillings to restore decayed or damaged teeth. Natural-looking results that blend seamlessly with your smile.

Tooth Extraction

Starting from ₹500

Safe, gentle extractions including wisdom teeth removal. Local anaesthesia ensures a comfortable experience. Sedation options available for anxious patients.

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Starting from ₹3,000

Modern, virtually painless RCT using rotary instrumentation to save infected teeth and eliminate pain. Most cases completed in 1–2 comfortable sessions.

Dental Crown

Starting from ₹1,200

Custom-fitted crowns in zirconia, PFM, or e-max porcelain to restore broken, cracked, or root-canal-treated teeth to full strength and natural appearance.

Braces & Aligners

Starting from ₹25,000

Straighten your smile with metal braces, ceramic braces, or discreet clear aligners. Customised orthodontic treatment plans for children, teens, and adults.

Paediatric Dentistry

Starting from ₹200

Gentle, child-friendly dental care in a welcoming, fun environment. Kids' checkups, fluoride treatments, and habit correction to build healthy smiles early.

Prices may vary depending on the case, complexity, and consultation findings.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've answered the most common ones below. Feel free to call us for anything else.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, your first consultation with us is completely free. This includes a dental examination and a personalised treatment plan with transparent pricing. No hidden costs.

Is root canal treatment painful?

With modern techniques and effective local anaesthesia, root canal treatment is no more uncomfortable than a routine filling. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable the experience is.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium post fuses with your jawbone and is incredibly durable. The crown on top may need replacement after 15–20 years.

How often should I get a dental checkup?

We recommend a routine checkup and professional cleaning every 6 months. This helps detect issues early and prevents more serious (and costly) problems down the line.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated paediatric dental area designed to make children comfortable. We recommend bringing children for their first dental visit by age 2.

What payment options are available?

We accept cash, all major debit/credit cards, UPI payments, and offer EMI options for larger treatments. Insurance claims assistance is also available for eligible patients.

Can I get same-day treatment?

Many treatments including fillings, cleanings, extractions, and teeth whitening can be completed on the same day. Emergency cases are prioritised for immediate treatment.

How long does teeth whitening last?

In-clinic whitening results typically last 1–3 years depending on lifestyle factors like coffee, tea, and smoking. With a take-home maintenance kit, results can be maintained much longer.
